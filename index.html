the demands project.

The Demands Project is centered on better understanding Black student experiences and needs on college campuses. Using the Black student demands that were created from over 80 Black student organizations, this project will explore emerging trends, institutional differences, and tactics of communication from these brave student activists.

LEARN MORE

project exploratory questions.

TRENDS

What are some of the overarching trends in the national Black student demands?

DISTINCTIONS

Are there institutional similarities or differences in the types of demands being presented? If so, what are they?

TACTICS

How did student activists communicate their demands (needs) to their their respective university administration?

data source.

The data source is from TheDemands.org. Collected by WetheProtesters, in partnership with the Black Liberation Collective, the data was collected in 2015 and 2016 as a compository for all Black student groups who were creating demands for the campuses administration.
